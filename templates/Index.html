Kitchen Recipes

Recipes, relishes, remedies and more...

  1. Using the Search Bar:
    • Recipe#: you can search by recipe number e.g. 5 or 12. Sometimes this may show a few unrelated results
    • Keyword: You can search by keyword e.g. "breakfast", "meat" or "sandwich"
    • Hall-of-Fame: some recipes have been battle-tested under the toughest conditions: a picky pallet and an even more selective tummy. Type 3 asterisks "***" to see these culinary delights
  2. Selecting A Dish:
    • Once your search results are display, scroll to your favorite dish and click to select
    • This will display the full details for the dish, including the name and cooking method
  3. Cooking & Eating:
    • You can use your stove, fire or whatever.
    • Most ingredients are relatively acceessible. Yea, we don't eat frogs legs on the regular either :-)
    • Sources: compiled by Amy Zimmermann from expererience, various of sources, and occassional contribution from Tich Mangono
